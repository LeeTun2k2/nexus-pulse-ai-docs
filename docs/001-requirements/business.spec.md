# Business Specifications for AI Content Generation System

## 1. Overview

- SaaS platform similar to Pippit AI.
- Generate images, videos, and voice from prompts, text, links, or uploads.
- Goal: Enable rapid content creation for content creators and marketers, reducing manual costs.

## 2. Target Audience

- Tier 1: Content Creators, Marketers.
- Tier 2: Small - Medium Brands.
- Initial Goal: 1000 users in 12 months, 10% free-to-paid conversion.
- Persona:
  - Persona 1: E-commerce owner (30-45 years old, online seller, needs quick product videos).
  - Persona 2: Social media marketer (25-35 years old, needs batch image/ad generation).

## 3. Monetization Flow

- Free tier to attract users (limited trial credits).
- Credit-based pricing.

## 5. Risks & Assumptions
- Assumption: SEA e-commerce market grows 20% annually with high demand for AI content.
- Risk: Competition from Pippit AI, Canva AI; Mitigation: Focus on low-cost, localized features (Vietnamese language support).
- Budget: Low cost for 1000 users.
